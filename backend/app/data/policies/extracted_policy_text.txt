Company Travel & Expense Policy 
 
Meals: 
Employees can claim meal expenses up to INR 2000 per day. 
Any expenses exceeding this limit will not be reimbursed. 
 
Transport: 
Taxi and travel expenses are allowed up to INR 3000 per trip. 
 
Lodging: 
Hotel accommodation is allowed up to INR 7000 per night. 
 
Restrictions: 
Alcohol, cigarettes, and personal entertainment expenses are strictly not reimbursable. 
 
General: 
All claims must include a valid receipt and correct date.
